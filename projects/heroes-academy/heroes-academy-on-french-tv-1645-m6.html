The Heroes Academy was on TV!

The Heroes Academy, one of the projects that I developed for Team Break, was shown on TV in the 19-45 show on the M6 ​​channel, in prime time.

For 2 minutes, the report explores the action games trend which is currently developing in France and of which the Heroes Academy is a perfect ambassador, mixing immersion, sport, reflection and adventure.

A project that I loved working on and which is constantly growing.
